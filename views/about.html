About us

Muhammad AlMahameed

I have over seven years of experience in research, teaching and accounting in Damascus, London and Birmingham. My research focuses on Social and Environmental Accounting as a part of my research vision of contributing to a body of knowledge of alternative accounting as an educational and informative mechanism that explores social justice, human rights and refugees’ crisis. My teaching involves delivering Financial and Management Accounting modules to accounting and non-accounting students. I have developed an interactive teaching tool ‘Play & Learn Accounting’ to facilitate the delivery of introductory accounting modules. I coach MSc students on leadership and team working skills.
